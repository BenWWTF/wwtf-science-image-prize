About the Prize

The WWTF Science Image Prize celebrates the visual power of research. We invite researchers from all disciplines to submit images that reveal the beauty, complexity, or wonder of their scientific work.

Winning images will be featured in the Vienna Research Radar, WWTF's annual report, press releases, and public exhibitions — with full attribution to you as the creator.

Who Can Apply
🎓

All career stages

PhD students, postdocs, junior and senior researchers — everyone is welcome.

🔬

All disciplines

Natural sciences, social sciences, humanities, engineering, medicine, arts — no field is excluded.

Life SciencesPhysicsSocial Sciences Computer ScienceHistoryEngineering Medicine& more
📍

Based in Vienna

You must be affiliated with a Vienna-based research institution at the time of submission.

What We're Looking For
🖼️

Visual impact

Images that stop you in your tracks — striking composition, unexpected beauty, or striking data visualization.

💡

Scientific meaning

The image should be rooted in real research and communicate something true about your field.

🌍

Accessibility

A non-specialist should be able to appreciate the image — even without knowing the science behind it.

Submission Guidelines
  • Up to 3 images per person
  • Formats: JPEG, PNG, TIFF, WebP
  • Maximum file size: 50 MB per image (high resolution preferred)
  • Each image needs a title, description, and method statement
  • You must be the copyright holder of all submitted images
  • No AI-generated images; post-processing is allowed

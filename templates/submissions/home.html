About

Celebrating the visual power of research

Winning images will be featured in the Vienna Research Radar, WWTF's annual report, press releases, and public exhibitions — with full attribution to you as creator.

Eligibility
🎓

All career stages

PhD students, postdocs, junior and senior researchers — everyone welcome.

🔬

All disciplines

Natural sciences, social sciences, humanities, engineering, medicine, arts — no field excluded.

Life SciencesPhysics Social SciencesComputer Science HistoryEngineering MedicineMathematics + more
📍

Vienna-based

Affiliated with a Vienna research institution at time of submission.

What we look for
🖼️

Visual impact

Images that stop you — striking composition, unexpected beauty, or powerful data visualization.

💡

Scientific meaning

Rooted in real research. Communicates something true about your field.

🌍

Accessibility

A non-specialist should be able to appreciate it — even without knowing the science.

Guidelines
Up to 3 images per submission
Formats: JPEG, PNG, TIFF, WebP · max 50 MB each
Each image needs a title, description, and creation method
You must hold copyright for all submitted images
No AI-generated images; post-processing allowed
How it works
1

Submit — Fill in your details and upload up to 3 images with metadata by 31 October.

2

Review — A jury of scientists and science communicators evaluates all entries.

3

Awarded — Winners announced at the WWTF annual event and receive their prizes in early 2026.

Takes about 10 minutes. Works on your phone.

About

Celebrating the visual power of research

Science creates images of extraordinary beauty and insight. The WWTF ReFrame puts them in front of Vienna and the world.

All submitted images may be featured in the Vienna Research Radar, WWTF's annual report, and public science communication activities — with full attribution to the researcher and institution.

Eligibility

Open to all Vienna researchers.

No field excluded, no career stage excluded. If you do research in Vienna, this prize is for you.

All career stages

PhD students, postdocs, junior and senior researchers, and professors — anyone actively doing research in Vienna.

All disciplines

Natural sciences, humanities, engineering, medicine, social sciences — every field of research is eligible.

Life SciencesPhysicsMedicineSocial SciencesComputer Science
Vienna-based

You must be affiliated with a Vienna research institution at the time of submission. No Austrian citizenship required.

Image guidelines

What makes a strong entry.

Your image should be both scientifically meaningful and visually compelling.

High resolution

Minimum 2 MP, ideally 10 MP or higher. JPEG, PNG, TIFF, or WebP.

Your own copyright

You must be the sole copyright holder. Images taken by or belonging to others are not eligible.

Scientifically grounded

Images must originate from your research — microscopy, simulations, field data, measurements, etc.

Minimal post-processing

Standard adjustments (brightness, contrast, white balance) are permitted. Composites or generative AI are not.

Process

How it works.

Step 01
Fill in your details

Name, institution, position, and research field. Takes about two minutes.

Step 02
Upload your images

Add 1–3 images with a title, description, and method for each. Works on mobile.

Step 03
Wait for the verdict

An independent jury evaluates all submissions. Results are announced after the deadline on 29 September 2026, 2 pm.

Ready to submit?

Takes about 10 minutes. Works on your phone. Deadline: 29 September 2026, 2 pm.
